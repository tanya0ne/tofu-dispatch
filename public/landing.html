FAQ
Have doubts?
"My workers aren't tech-savvy — they won't use another app." +
There is no other app. They get a WhatsApp message — the same app they already use every day. They reply like they'd reply to you. If they can text, they can use Tofu Dispatch. We've seen response rates above 80% from crews who barely use email.
"I don't want a bot talking to my workers." +
Neither do we. Workers see messages from your business — not from "Tofu Bot." The tone matches how you'd write. And Tofu Dispatch never makes decisions on its own: reschedules, reassignments, anything important — it asks you first. You approve, then it sends.
"What if it sends wrong information?" +
Every message is built from your actual Tofu FSM schedule — real job data, real addresses, real times. No AI hallucinations. No guessing. If your schedule is accurate, the messages are accurate. You can review message templates before anything goes out.
"I need to stay in control." +
That's the whole point. Tofu Dispatch automates the delivery — not the decisions. Reminders and status collection run automatically. But changes, reschedules, and anything that requires judgment always goes through you. Cruise control, not autopilot.
